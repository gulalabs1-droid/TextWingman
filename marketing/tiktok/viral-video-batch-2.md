# Text Wingman TikTok Viral Batch 2

Goal: make the next batch feel native to TikTok, not like SaaS ads. Every post should earn attention first, then sell the tool after the viewer already wants the answer.

## Posting Formula

Post 5/day. Each day should include:

- 2 pain posts: make the viewer feel the exact texting panic.
- 1 proof/demo post: show Text Wingman solving it.
- 1 comment-bait post: ask viewers to drop the text they got.
- 1 identity/funny post: "this is you when..." content people share.

Use TikTok-native audio at upload. Do not bake music into the MP4. Pick a rising or familiar sound inside TikTok, keep it low, and let on-screen text do the selling.

Measure each post after 24 hours:

- Hook hold: views versus 2-second retention.
- Intent: profile visits and link clicks.
- Demand: comments with real texts.
- Sales signal: paste/upload starts on `/tiktok`, first result generated, copy reply.
